The connection is called the Pythagorean Theorem and got its name from the Greek mathematician Pythagoras, who lived about 2,500 years ago. Let's see what the Pythagorean theorem says. If the lengths of the catheters in a right-angled triangle are a and b, and the length of the hypotenuse is c, then the relation is a² + b² = c²
